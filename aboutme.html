hello, I'm Syahirah or call me caa instead can you?

On the 25 June 2003, a pair of beautiful and sweet girls were born. Nur Syahirah and Nur Sabrina were the name given after registering the birth certificate at the Petaling Jaya National Registration Department. I'm the older sister and ina (sabrina) is the younger sister. The twins and I were born at the University Malaya Medical Center (PPUM) accompanied by my father and also his collegues came to visit because they both worked at the university.

What I want to tell you is that all sweet childhood memories are the most precious memories for me because of my beloved parents. However, the happy times did not last forever as dreamed. My parents separated when I was 3 or 4 years old probably because my memory is not strong enough to remember those dark memories. Fortunately, my father told me when I was an adult and the wounds gradually healed over time.

I was born in the state of the federal territory and lived only for short period of time because my father's office moved to the branch of the University of Malaya in Kelantan, but there it was specifically for Islamic studies academics. Overall I grew up in Kelantan with my other siblings. In these siblings, starting with my third brother up to my twin who was born in the city state. While the eldest and second were born in Kelantan. From the beginning, my family moved around because of my father's job change. After all, my father's hometown is in Kelantan so maybe that's the main reason haha.
